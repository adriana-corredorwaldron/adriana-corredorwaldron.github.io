Adriana Corredor-Waldron

Welcome!

I am an economist working on topics related to health policy. I am interested in understanding how we can care better for people with long-term needs, such as those living with mental health conditions and disabilities. My recent work studies the role of substance abuse treatment clinics on ER use.

I am a Postdoctoral Research Associate at Princeton University's Center for Health and Wellbeing. I received my Ph.D. in Economics from University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign, and my B.A. from Universidad de los Andes.
