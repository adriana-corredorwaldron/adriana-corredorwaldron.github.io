Adriana Corredor-Waldron

Welcome!

I am an economist working on topics related to health policy. I am especially interested in policies that impact individuals with long-term needs, such as those living with a mental health condition or a physical disability. My recent work studies the effect of access to substance abuse treatment on ER use.

I am a Postdoctoral Research Associate at Princeton University's Center for Health and Wellbeing. I received my Ph.D. in Economics from University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign, and my B.A. from Universidad de los Andes.
